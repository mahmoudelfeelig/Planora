Key limitations and threats to validity:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Synthetic data bias.} Most results are on generated instances; real institutional datasets may require additional constraints (travel times, precedence chains, split rooms, instructor preferences beyond current model).
  \item \textbf{Short strict budgets.} The strict-room attempt budget in our reported batch is intentionally short, which favors fallback behavior on larger scenarios.
  \item \textbf{Greedy-room decoupling.} In \texttt{room\_mode=greedy}, room constraints do not guide CP time assignment; room infeasibility can still appear at extraction time in tightly constrained cases.
  \item \textbf{Local-search maturity.} The current improver includes bad-entity prioritization (worst groups/staff first), deterministic group/week sweeps, bounded 2-step compound lookahead, local-delta scoring, and bounded restarts, but it is still single-solution local search; larger compound neighborhoods and benchmark-tuned parameter control remain future work.
  \item \textbf{Small seed set.} Three seeds per scenario are sufficient for regression checks, but not for strong statistical claims.
\end{itemize}

Planned next steps are:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item ingestion of public/real datasets with explicit schema mapping,
  \item stronger quality optimization neighborhoods and ablation studies,
  \item richer infeasibility explanations derived from unsatisfied core constraints.
\end{itemize}
